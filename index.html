What's The Mystery Event?

Book 1 of the Petal Crew Series

Something mysterious is happening at Higgsfield Academy — and only Sevyn and her crew have what it takes to figure it out.

With Sevyn's vision, Bloom's heart, Lux's sharp mind, Reign's strategy, and Core's creativity, this team of five extraordinary kids discovers that the greatest superpower of all is knowing who you are.

A story about friendship, courage, and the magic that happens when everyone brings their best to the table.

MEET THE AUTHOR

The Woman Behind the Magic

Shannor Williams is a storyteller, entrepreneur, and community builder whose work spans luxury event design, youth empowerment, and creative education.

As the founder of The Sevyn Experience, Shannor has built a multi-faceted creative brand rooted in one belief: that extraordinary is available to everyone. Sevyn & The Petal Crew was born from her passion for giving every child a character they can see themselves in — brilliant, purposeful, and unstoppable.

When she's not writing, Shannor is designing unforgettable events, hosting her podcast The Power Behind the Beauty, and running The Young Experience — a youth enrichment program empowering the next generation of leaders.
